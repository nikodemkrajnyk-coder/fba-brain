Our Story

A family tradition since 1998

🍝

From Naples to London

Founded by Chef Marco Rossi, Bella Cucina brings the authentic flavours of Southern Italy to the heart of London. Every dish is crafted with love, using recipes passed down through three generations of the Rossi family.

We source our ingredients directly from small Italian farms — from San Marzano tomatoes to buffalo mozzarella from Campania. Our pasta is made fresh daily, and our pizzas are baked in a traditional wood-fired oven imported from Naples.

Whether you're joining us for a romantic dinner, a family celebration, or a casual lunch, we promise an unforgettable dining experience.
