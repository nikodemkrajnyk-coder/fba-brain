Overview

Bambu Indah is a radically different hotel experience in Ubud, Bali. Eleven antique Javanese bridal homes have been lovingly restored and transformed into unique, luxurious eco-cabins set amongst organic gardens, natural springs, and rice paddies.

🌱 Eco-Certified 👥 Community-Run ⚡ Solar Powered

What Makes It Special

The property runs its own permaculture farm, uses solar energy, and employs 95% local staff. Guests can join cooking classes using ingredients grown on-site, visit the community school funded by the hotel, or simply relax by the stunning natural pool.

Prices & Booking

Rooms from £120/night including breakfast. Book direct through the property for the best rate and to ensure your money goes directly to the local team.
